About the Hackathon

The Green AI Hackathon will take place on December 20-21, 2025, at the National School of Advanced Sciences and Technologies (ENSTA) in Borj Cedria.

During the competition, participants will deliver a short presentation, including a real-time demonstration, to convince a diverse audience of the value of their AI project. Their presentation should highlight the following aspects: the motivation behind their project, the approach and methods used, the impact of their solution, and the sustainability of the results achieved.

Target Audience

The hackathon is open exclusively to students from the University of Carthage who are passionate about artificial intelligence and innovation:

  • Students: from all disciplines within the University of Carthage, looking to develop their skills and apply their knowledge.
  • Researchers: affiliated with the University of Carthage and leading applied or exploratory projects related to artificial intelligence, particularly in the field of Green AI.

Participants may register in teams of 2 to 4 members.
